New Introducing Organizational Intelligence Lab

Organizational Intelligence Lab is an artificial intelligence research and product company. We are building a future where people can collaborate with AI more easily and more safely across a wide range of use cases.

AI capabilities are improving rapidly, yet meaningful collaboration with AI still requires significant effort. Teams often rely on repeated inference to identify where systems behave reliably, or large amounts of data to expand those reliable regions. We approach this challenge by organizing intelligence. Our aim is to shape AI systems whose knowledge is structured so that collaboration becomes more natural, predictable, and safe.

Well-organized knowledge is easier to use, faster to apply, and safer to scale.

We help AI learn knowledge that is clear and simple. When knowledge is explicit and structured, it becomes easier to reuse, easier to extend, and easier to understand how different components influence one another. This clarity leads to more stable behavior and ultimately better performance.

We also help teams access that knowledge. This includes examining model internals to understand how information is represented, as well as providing tools that evaluate how reliably specific functionality emerges within a model. These capabilities make it possible to assess whether AI systems are ready for real-world deployment, including safety requirements and regulatory considerations.
